About Me

I am a final-year undergraduate Computer Science student at the University of Toronto, and currently a Research Intern at Stanford HAI under the supervision of Dr. Jiaxin Pei. It is my pleasure to have worked as a Research Intern at the Vector Institute, collaborating with Prof. Wenhu Chen.

Starting Fall 2026, I will join the School of Information at the University of Texas at Austin as a Ph.D. student.

I am working on:

User-Centric Agent Application
  • Build a benchmark and framework for multi-user LLM agent interactions [Multi-User]
  • Reveal risks in agent-to-agent negotiation and transactions in consumer markets [A2A-NT]
Socially Responsible AI
  • Benchmark LLM robustness against augmented fraud and phishing inducements [Fraud-R1]
  • Develop adaptive jailbreak detection for vision-language models [JailDAM]
  • Examine the fidelity of AI in representing user voices for large-scale opinion deliberation [Deliberation]
AI Auditing and Governance
  • Build a scalable framework for auditing system prompts in user-centric LLMs [AISPA]

If you share similar interests or would like to chat, feel free to reach out at shenzhe@utexas.edu.
