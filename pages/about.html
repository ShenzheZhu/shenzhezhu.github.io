About Me

I am a 4th-year undergraduate Computer Science student at the University of Toronto, and currently a Research Intern at Stanford HAI under the supervision of Dr. Jiaxin Pei. Starting Fall 2025, I will join the Vector Institute as a Research Intern. I will work with Prof. Wenhu Chen to develop effective frameworks for agentic AI training.

My research centers on building human-centered, reliable AI systems grounded in social responsibility. My current interests include:

  1. Effective Human-AI Collaboration: Designing human AI interaction paradigms that preserve human agency, shared control, and reliability in real world decision making.
  2. Realistic evaluation and AI infrastructure: Building realistic training and evaluation environments, datasets, and simulations to stress test AI systems under real world uncertainty.
  3. AI auditing and governance: Developing scalable auditing methods and governance frameworks for reliable and accountable AI deployment.

If you share similar interests or would like to chat, feel free to reach out at shenzhez@mit.edu.
