About Me

I am a 3rd-year undergraduate Computer Science student at the University of Toronto, and currently a Research Intern at Stanford HAI under the supervision of Dr. Jiaxin Pei. Starting Fall 2025, I will join the Vector Institute as a Research Intern. I will work with Prof. Wenhu Chen to develop effective frameworks for agentic AI training.

My research centers on developing trustworthy and socially responsible AI, with a focus on user-centered challenges such as bias, risk, and security vulnerabilities. Most recently, I am pretty interested in building reliable LLM agents to foster user trust.
