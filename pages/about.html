About Me

I am a final-year undergraduate Computer Science student at the University of Toronto, and currently a Research Intern at Stanford HAI under the supervision of Dr. Jiaxin Pei. It is my pleasure to have worked as a Research Intern at the Vector Institute, collaborating with Prof. Wenhu Chen.

Starting Fall 2026, I will join the School of Information at the University of Texas at Austin as a Ph.D. student.

My research centers on building human-centered, reliable AI systems grounded in social responsibility, including:

  1. Effective Human-AI Collaboration: Designing human AI interaction paradigms that preserve human agency, shared control, and reliability in real world decision making.
  2. Realistic AI Evaluation and Infrastructure: Building realistic training and evaluation environments, datasets, and simulations to stress test AI systems under real world uncertainty.
  3. AI Auditing and Governance: Developing scalable auditing methods and governance frameworks for reliable and accountable AI deployment.

If you share similar interests or would like to chat, feel free to reach out at shenzhez@mit.edu.
