# 🤔 About Me
<span class='anchor' id='about-me'></span>
I am a 3rd-year Computer Science student at the <a href='https://www.utoronto.ca/'>University of Toronto</a>. Before switching professions to research, I worked as a data analyst for a period of time. It was a great pleasure and honor to collaborate with the <a href='https://pradalab1.github.io/research.html'>PRADA Lab</a> at King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST), which gave me my first experience and guidance in academic research.

Currently, my research centers on developing trustworthy and socially responsible AI, with a focus on human-centered challenges such as bias, risk, and security vulnerabilities. Most recently, I have been particularly interested in building reliable LLM agents for social good, closely collaborating with <a href='https://jiaxin-pei.github.io/'>Dr. Jiaxin Pei</a> from the Stanford HAI<a href='https://hai.stanford.edu/'>Stanford Digital Economy Lab</a> and <a href='https://digitaleconomy.stanford.edu/'>Digital Economy Lab</a>.
